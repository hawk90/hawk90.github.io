  \textbf{결과}: 매 write마다 cross-core coherence traffic. 측정 시 10$\sim$100배 slowdown 흔함.
};
\node[note, anchor=north, fill=okfill, draw=okbord, text width=160mm, align=left] at (0, -5.6) {
