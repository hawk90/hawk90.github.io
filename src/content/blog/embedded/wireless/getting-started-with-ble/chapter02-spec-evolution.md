> Outline — *4.0/4.1/4.2* — BLE 도입, LE Secure Connections, Data Length Extension. *5.0* — 2M PHY (2× 속도), Coded PHY S=2/S=8 (4× 거리), Advertising Extensions (>255B). *5.1* — Direction Finding (AoA/AoD), GATT Caching. *5.2* — LE Audio (Isochronous Channels). *5.3* — periodic adv enhancement. *5.4* — PAwR (Periodic Advertising with Responses). 책은 5.0 기준이지만 5.x 차이도 정리.
