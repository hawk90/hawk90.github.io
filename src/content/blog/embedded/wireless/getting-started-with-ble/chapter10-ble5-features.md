> Outline — *2M PHY* — 2 Msymbol/s (2× throughput, 같은 거리). *Coded PHY* — S=2 (2× 거리, 1/2 속도), S=8 (4× 거리, 1/8 속도). *Extended Advertising* — payload >31B (최대 1650B via chained). *Advertising sets* — 여러 adv 동시. *Periodic Advertising* — 동기화된 주기 광고 (sync to beacon). *LE Audio (5.2)* — Isochronous Channels, LC3 codec, broadcast audio (Auracast). *Direction Finding (5.1)* — AoA/AoD via antenna array.
