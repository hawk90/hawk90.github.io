> Outline — *Multi-slave* — independent CS vs daisy. *Dual SPI* — 2 data lane. *Quad SPI (QSPI)* — 4 lane, NOR flash 인터페이스 표준. *Octo-SPI (OctSPI)* — 8 lane, DDR mode. *XIP (eXecute In Place)*. *Memory-mapped mode* — CPU가 flash를 직접 read. 모던 MCU에서 boot 매체.
