> Outline — *Zynq-7000 SoC* — *PS* (Processing System, dual Cortex-A9) + *PL* (Programmable Logic). *Zynq UltraScale+ MPSoC* — quad Cortex-A53 + dual Cortex-R5F + Mali GPU + PL. *왜 SoC + FPGA인가* — high-throughput preprocessing + general purpose CPU. SW 관점에서 PL은 *가속기*로 본다. PS-PL 통신 — AXI 다음 장.
