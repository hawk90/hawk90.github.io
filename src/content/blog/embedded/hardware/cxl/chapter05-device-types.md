> Outline — *Type 1* — CXL.io + CXL.cache (NIC·smart accelerator, 자체 memory 없음). *Type 2* — CXL.io + CXL.cache + CXL.mem (GPU·FPGA accelerator with HBM). *Type 3* — CXL.io + CXL.mem (memory expander). 각 타입의 *bias mode*·*coherency 책임* 차이. 실제 제품 예 — Astera Leo, SK hynix CMM, Samsung MS1.
