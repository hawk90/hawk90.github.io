> Outline — *system requirements* → *HLR* (High-Level Requirements, *what*) → *LLR* (Low-Level Requirements, *how*). *Derived requirements* — 상위에 없지만 구현이 도출하는 것 (system safety assessment에 feedback 필요). 요구사항 *attribute* — unique ID, verifiable, atomic, consistent. DOORS/Polarion/Jama 같은 RM 도구.
