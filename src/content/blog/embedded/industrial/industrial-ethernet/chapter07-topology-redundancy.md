> Outline — *토폴로지* — line (단순, 끊김 위험), star (스위치 비싸짐), ring (이중화 OK), tree (대규모). *MRP (Media Redundancy Protocol)* — PROFINET, 200ms recovery. *DLR (Device Level Ring)* — EtherNet/IP, 3ms recovery. *PRP (Parallel Redundancy Protocol)* — 동시 두 경로 송신, 0ms recovery. *HSR (High-availability Seamless Redundancy)* — ring + 0ms. *IEC 62439-3*. 선택 기준 — recovery time vs 대역폭 비용.
