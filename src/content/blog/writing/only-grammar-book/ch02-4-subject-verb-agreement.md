> 주어가 *단수*이면 동사도 *단수*, 주어가 *복수*이면 동사도 *복수*. 단순한 원칙이지만 *까다로운 자리*가 많습니다.

## 정의 및 개요

주어-동사 일치(subject-verb agreement)는 영어의 *기본 규칙*입니다. 한국어에는 없는 변별이므로 한국어 화자가 *자주 빠뜨리는* 자리입니다.

```text
She walks every day.       (3인칭 단수 — walks)
They walk every day.        (복수 — walk)
```

기본은 단순합니다. 그러나 *주어가 길거나, 집합명사이거나, 부정대명사일 때* 결정이 까다로워집니다.

## 기본 규칙

### 인칭과 수에 따른 동사 변화

| 주어 | 일반 동사 | be 동사 (현재) | be 동사 (과거) | have |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| I | walk | am | was | have |
| You | walk | are | were | have |
| He / She / It | walks | is | was | has |
| We | walk | are | were | have |
| They | walk | are | were | have |

3인칭 단수 *현재 시제*에서 동사에 *-s*. 가장 자주 빠뜨리는 자리.

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | She walk to school. |
| 바름 | She walks to school. |
| 회피 | The dog bark loudly. |
| 바름 | The dog barks loudly. |

## 까다로운 주어

### 1. 주어와 동사 사이의 구·절

전치사구·관계절이 주어와 동사 사이에 끼어들 때 *진짜 주어*에 일치.

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | The book on the shelves are mine. |
| 바름 | The book on the shelves is mine. (주어 = book — 단수) |
| 회피 | The students who attended the conference was tired. |
| 바름 | The students who attended the conference were tired. (주어 = students — 복수) |

점검 — 주어와 동사 사이의 *구·절*을 *제외*하고 봅니다.

### 2. 등위접속사로 연결된 주어

#### *and*로 연결 — 복수 취급

```text
Tom and Jerry are friends.
The teacher and the student have a meeting.
```

예외 — *and*로 연결되지만 *하나의 단위*를 가리키면 단수.

```text
Bread and butter is my favorite breakfast.
Rock and roll is my favorite music.
```

#### *or, nor*로 연결 — 가까운 주어에 일치

```text
Either the manager or the employees are responsible.
Either the employees or the manager is responsible.
Neither the teacher nor the students were present.
Neither the students nor the teacher was present.
```

### 3. 부정대명사

#### 단수 취급

```text
each, either, neither, every, one, anyone, anybody, anything,
everyone, everybody, everything, someone, somebody, something,
no one, nobody, nothing
```

```text
Everyone is welcome.
Each of the students has a desk.
Nobody knows the answer.
Either of these options is acceptable.
```

#### 복수 취급

```text
both, few, many, several, others
```

```text
Both are correct.
Few were absent.
Many were disappointed.
```

#### 단수 또는 복수 (지칭에 따라)

```text
all, any, more, most, none, some, half, part, the majority, the rest
```

```text
All of the cake is gone.        (cake — 불가산 단수)
All of the cookies are gone.    (cookies — 복수)
Some of the water is dirty.     (water — 단수)
Some of the apples are rotten.  (apples — 복수)
Half of the team is here.       (team — 단수)
Half of the players are here.   (players — 복수)
```

### 4. 집합명사

| 명사 | 동사 |
|---|---|
| family, team, committee, jury, audience, government, staff | 미국식 단수, 영국식 복수도 가능 |

```text
미국식: The team is winning.
영국식: The team are winning. (구성원 강조)
```

학술 글은 *미국식 단수*가 표준.

### 5. 수량 표현

#### 단위로서의 양 — 단수

```text
Five dollars is a lot to me.
Ten miles is too far to walk.
Twenty years is a long time.
```

수량이 *하나의 단위*로 인식되면 단수.

#### 분수·백분율 — 명사에 일치

```text
Two-thirds of the cake is gone.        (cake — 단수)
Two-thirds of the students are absent. (students — 복수)
Fifty percent of the work is done.     (work — 단수)
Fifty percent of the workers are absent. (workers — 복수)
```

### 6. *the number of* vs *a number of*

- **the number of** — 단수 (수 자체)
- **a number of** — 복수 (여러 명)

```text
The number of students has increased.
A number of students are absent today.
```

### 7. 도치 — *there is / there are*

진주어는 *be* 뒤에. 진주어에 일치.

```text
There is a book on the table.
There are three books on the table.
```

회피 — *There's three books* (구어에서 흔하지만 격식 글에서는 회피)

### 8. 관계대명사절의 동사

관계대명사가 가리키는 *선행사*에 일치.

```text
The man who is calling is my uncle.
The men who are calling are my uncles.

She is one of those people who always complain.
       (선행사 = people — 복수)
```

### 9. *Each, every* + 명사

단수 명사 + 단수 동사.

```text
Each student has a desk.
Every book in the library is catalogued.
```

여러 주어를 *and*로 연결해도 *each, every*가 앞이면 단수.

```text
Each boy and (each) girl has a chance.
Every man, woman, and child was present.
```

### 10. 일부 명사는 -s 형태지만 단수

```text
news, economics, mathematics, physics, politics, statistics,
ethics, athletics, gymnastics, measles, mumps
```

```text
The news is shocking.
Mathematics is my favorite subject.
Politics is a complex field.
```

단, *statistics*나 *politics*가 *통계 자료, 정치적 견해*를 가리킬 때는 복수.

```text
Statistics show a clear trend.   (통계 수치 — 복수)
Statistics is a useful subject.  (학문 — 단수)
```

### 11. 항상 복수인 명사

쌍을 이루는 명사 — 항상 복수.

```text
scissors, pants, trousers, jeans, shorts, glasses, binoculars, pliers
```

```text
These scissors are sharp.
My jeans are dirty.
```

*a pair of*를 붙이면 단수.

```text
A pair of scissors is on the desk.
```

### 12. *one of …* vs *the only one of …*

- **one of [복수 명사] who/that + 복수 동사**: 여러 명 중 하나
- **the only one of [복수 명사] who/that + 단수 동사**: 유일한 한 명

```text
She is one of the students who study hard.
        (학생들 모두 열심히 공부)

She is the only one of the students who studies hard.
        (그녀만 열심히 공부)
```

## 자주 보는 함정

### 1. 주어와 동사 사이의 명사에 일치

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | The list of items are on the desk. |
| 바름 | The list of items is on the desk. (주어 = list) |

### 2. *each*, *every*, *neither* 복수 처리

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | Each of the students have a desk. |
| 바름 | Each of the students has a desk. |
| 회피 | Neither answer are correct. |
| 바름 | Neither answer is correct. |

### 3. *there is / there are* 도치

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | There is many problems. |
| 바름 | There are many problems. |

### 4. *the number of* / *a number of*

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | The number of students are increasing. |
| 바름 | The number of students is increasing. |
| 회피 | A number of students is absent. |
| 바름 | A number of students are absent. |

### 5. 집합명사

```text
회피 (미국): The team are winning.
바름 (미국): The team is winning.
```

### 6. *news, mathematics* 같은 -s 단수

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | The news are shocking. |
| 바름 | The news is shocking. |
| 회피 | Mathematics are difficult. |
| 바름 | Mathematics is difficult. |

## 정리

- 기본 — 3인칭 단수 현재에 *-s*
- 주어와 동사 사이의 구·절은 *제외*하고 일치
- *and*로 연결한 주어는 복수, *or/nor*는 가까운 주어에 일치
- 부정대명사 — *each, every, anyone* 단수, *both, many* 복수
- *all, some, most* — 지칭하는 명사에 일치
- 집합명사 — 미국식 단수가 표준
- 수량의 *단위*는 단수, *the number of* 단수 / *a number of* 복수
- *news, mathematics* 등 *-s* 형태의 단수 명사 주의
