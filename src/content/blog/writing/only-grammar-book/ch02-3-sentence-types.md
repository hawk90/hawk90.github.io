> 문장은 *독립절과 종속절의 결합*에 따라 *네 가지 종류*로 분류됩니다. 어떤 구조를 쓰느냐가 글의 *리듬과 명료성*을 결정합니다.

## 정의 및 개요

영어 문장의 *구조적 분류*는 *독립절(IC)*과 *종속절(DC)*의 결합 방식에 기반합니다.

| 종류 | 구성 | 약자 |
|---|---|---|
| 단순문 (Simple) | IC 1개 | IC |
| 중문 (Compound) | IC + IC | IC + IC |
| 복문 (Complex) | IC + DC | IC + DC |
| 중복문 (Compound-complex) | IC + IC + DC | IC + IC + DC |

같은 정보를 *네 가지 모두*로 표현할 수 있습니다. 어떤 구조를 선택하느냐가 *문체와 강조*를 결정합니다.

## 단순문 (Simple Sentence)

*하나의 독립절*만으로 이루어진 문장.

```text
She arrived.
The dog barked loudly.
My brother and his friend went to the movies.
```

### 단순문의 변형

단순문도 *복합 주어, 복합 동사, 다양한 구*를 가질 수 있습니다.

```text
복합 주어: Tom and Jerry are friends.
복합 동사: She sang and danced.
복합 주어 + 복합 동사: Tom and Jerry sang and danced.
+ 구: In the morning, Tom and Jerry sang and danced at the party.
```

여전히 *독립절 1개*입니다.

### 효과

- *명료함, 직접성*
- 짧은 문장으로 *강조*
- 초보자에게 안전한 구조

```text
She failed. The exam was hard.   (단순문 두 개로 강조)
```

너무 많이 쓰면 *유아적*이거나 *단조*롭게 들립니다.

## 중문 (Compound Sentence)

*두 개 이상의 독립절*이 *등위접속사*나 *세미콜론*으로 연결된 문장.

### 등위접속사로 연결

```text
She studied hard, but she failed the exam.
The rain stopped, and we went out for a walk.
You can stay here, or you can come with us.
```

*독립절 + 쉼표 + FANBOYS + 독립절*.

### 세미콜론으로 연결

```text
She likes coffee; he prefers tea.
The road was long; we were tired.
```

두 절이 *의미적으로 가까울 때*.

### 세미콜론 + 접속 부사

```text
The plan was risky; however, we agreed to try it.
She was tired; nevertheless, she finished the work.
```

*접속 부사*(however, therefore, moreover 등)는 *접속사가 아니므로* 쉼표만으로 두 절을 잇지 못합니다.

### 효과

- *대등한 두 생각*의 균형
- 인과·대비 관계의 명시
- 자연스러운 흐름

```text
She tried hard, but she failed.   (대비)
He was tired, so he went to bed.   (인과)
```

## 복문 (Complex Sentence)

*하나의 독립절 + 하나 이상의 종속절*.

```text
When she arrived, we started the meeting.
We started the meeting because she arrived.
The book that I read yesterday was excellent.
She is the woman who lives next door.
```

### 종속절의 위치

종속절은 *문장의 앞·중간·끝* 어디든 올 수 있습니다.

```text
앞: Although it was raining, we went out.
끝: We went out although it was raining.
중간: The man who lives next door is a doctor.
```

### 쉼표 규칙

- 종속절이 *앞*에 → 쉼표
- 종속절이 *뒤*에 → 쉼표 없음 (일반적)
- 중간 종속절이 *비제한적* → 쉼표로 분리
- 중간 종속절이 *제한적* → 쉼표 없음

```text
Because she was tired, she went to bed.   (앞 — 쉼표)
She went to bed because she was tired.   (뒤 — 쉼표 없음)
My brother, who lives in Seoul, called.  (비제한 — 쉼표)
The man who called is my uncle.          (제한 — 쉼표 없음)
```

### 효과

- *주된 정보*와 *보조 정보*의 명확한 구분
- 종속절로 *시간, 원인, 조건, 양보* 등 관계 표현
- 학술·논증 글에서 핵심

```text
Although the experiment failed, the data provided valuable insights.
```

## 중복문 (Compound-Complex Sentence)

*두 개 이상의 독립절 + 하나 이상의 종속절*.

```text
When the rain stopped, we went out, and we had a great time.
[DC: When the rain stopped] + [IC: we went out] + [IC: we had a great time]

She studied hard because she wanted to succeed, but she still failed.
[IC: She studied hard] + [DC: because she wanted to succeed] + [IC: she still failed]
```

### 효과

- *복잡한 관계*의 표현
- 학술·문학 글에서 *풍부한 구조*
- 너무 자주 쓰면 *읽기 어려움*

## 구조 비교

같은 내용을 네 가지 구조로 표현.

```text
정보: 비가 왔다. 우리는 집에 있었다. 영화를 봤다.

단순문 (3개):
It rained. We stayed home. We watched a movie.

중문:
It rained, so we stayed home and watched a movie.

복문:
Because it rained, we stayed home and watched a movie.

중복문:
Because it rained, we stayed home, and we watched a movie.
```

각 구조가 *다른 강조와 흐름*을 만듭니다.

## 문장 구조의 다양성

좋은 글은 *네 가지 구조를 섞어* 사용합니다. 같은 구조의 연속은 *단조롭게* 들립니다.

### 단조로운 예 (모두 단순문)

```text
She went to the store. She bought some milk. She came home.
She made breakfast. She ate it quickly.
```

### 다양한 예

```text
She went to the store, where she bought some milk. When she came home,
she made breakfast and ate it quickly.
```

같은 정보가 *훨씬 자연스럽게* 흐릅니다.

### 강조와 변형

짧은 단순문은 *강조*에 효과적입니다.

```text
She had tried everything — the diet, the exercise, the therapy. Nothing worked.
She was exhausted. But she didn't give up.
```

긴 복문 사이의 *짧은 단순문*이 *충격*과 *강조*를 만듭니다.

## 기능별 문장 분류

구조 외에 *기능*에 따른 분류도 있습니다.

| 종류 | 기능 | 예 | 구두점 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Declarative | 진술 | She is here. | . |
| Interrogative | 의문 | Is she here? | ? |
| Imperative | 명령 | Close the door. | . 또는 ! |
| Exclamatory | 감탄 | What a day! | ! |

대부분의 글은 *declarative*가 대다수.

## 예문

```text
단순문:
The deadline is Friday.

중문:
The deadline is Friday, but the team needs more time.

복문:
Although the deadline is Friday, the team needs more time.

중복문:
Although the deadline is Friday, the team needs more time,
and the manager has agreed to an extension.
```

```text
The report, which she submitted yesterday, has been reviewed,
and the committee approved it without any changes.

(중복문: IC + DC + IC, 비제한적 종속절 포함)
```

## 자주 보는 함정

### 1. Run-on 문장

종지부 없이 절들을 *나열*.

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | She studied hard she still failed. |
| 바름 | She studied hard, but she still failed. |
| 바름 | She studied hard; she still failed. |
| 바름 | She studied hard. She still failed. |

### 2. Comma splice

두 독립절을 *쉼표만으로* 연결.

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | She came, he left. |
| 바름 | She came, and he left. |
| 바름 | She came; he left. |
| 바름 | She came. He left. |

### 3. 종속절 단편

종속절을 *단독으로*.

| 분류 | 예문 |
|---|---|
| 회피 | Because it was raining. |
| 바름 | Because it was raining, we stayed home. |

### 4. 너무 긴 중복문

```text
회피: When the report that the team had been working on for weeks
was finally submitted, the manager, who had been impatient throughout
the project, accepted it without comment, but the team members,
who had expected praise, were disappointed.

바름: The team had worked on the report for weeks. When it was finally
submitted, the manager — who had been impatient throughout — accepted
it without comment. The team members had expected praise. They were
disappointed.
```

### 5. 모든 문장이 같은 구조

```text
회피: I woke up early. I ate breakfast. I went to work.
I came home. I made dinner. I watched TV.

바름: I woke up early and ate a quick breakfast before going to work.
When I came home, I made dinner and watched TV.
```

## 정리

- 네 가지 문장 종류 — *단순·중문·복문·중복문*
- 단순문 — IC 1개
- 중문 — IC + IC (쉼표 + FANBOYS 또는 세미콜론)
- 복문 — IC + DC (종속절 앞에 오면 쉼표)
- 중복문 — IC + IC + DC
- 좋은 글은 *네 가지 구조를 섞어* 사용
- 짧은 단순문은 *강조*에 효과적
