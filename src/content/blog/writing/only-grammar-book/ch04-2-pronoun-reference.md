> 대명사는 *선행사*(antecedent)를 가리킵니다. 어느 명사를 가리키는지 *분명하지 않으면* 문장이 모호해집니다.

## 정의 및 개요

대명사 참조(pronoun reference) 오류는 *대명사가 어느 명사를 가리키는지 불분명*할 때 발생합니다. 한국어는 *대명사를 자주 생략*하는 언어이므로 한국어 화자는 영어 대명사 사용 자체가 어색할 수 있습니다.

핵심 원칙은 *모든 대명사가 분명한 선행사를 가져야* 한다는 것입니다.

## 모호한 참조 (Ambiguous Reference)

### 두 명사 중 어느 것인지 불분명

```text
회피: When Mary met Susan, she was tired.   (she = Mary? Susan?)
바름: When Mary met Susan, Mary was tired.
바름: Mary was tired when she met Susan.    (she = Mary 명확)
```

### 멀리 떨어진 선행사

대명사와 선행사가 *너무 멀면* 참조가 약해집니다.

```text
회피: The book was on the table. The cat was sleeping under the table.
       The lamp was bright. It had been there for years.
       (it = book? table? cat? lamp?)
바름: ... The lamp had been there for years.   (명사 반복)
```

### 형용사·소유격을 선행사로

엄밀히 말하면 *명사*가 아니어서 모호.

```text
회피: In Lincoln's speech, he addressed the nation.
       (he의 선행사가 Lincoln인지 Lincoln's인지 모호)
바름: In his speech, Lincoln addressed the nation.
바름: Lincoln, in his speech, addressed the nation.
```

## 막연한 *this, that, it, which*

*문장 전체의 의미*를 가리키는 *this, that, it, which*는 격식 글에서 회피.

### 회피 — *this/that*만 단독

```text
회피: The project failed due to poor planning, lack of resources,
       and miscommunication. This led to layoffs.
       (this = ?)
바름: The project failed due to poor planning, lack of resources,
       and miscommunication. These failures led to layoffs.
바름: The project failed due to poor planning, lack of resources,
       and miscommunication. This failure led to layoffs.
```

*this/that* 뒤에 *명사*를 붙여 *무엇을* 가리키는지 명시.

### 회피 — *which*가 막연

```text
회피: She missed the deadline, which caused problems.
       (which = ?)
바름: She missed the deadline, an error that caused problems.
바름: She missed the deadline. This delay caused problems.
```

### 회피 — *it*이 막연

```text
회피: In the article it says that climate change is accelerating.
       (it = ?)
바름: The article says that climate change is accelerating.
```

## 대명사-선행사 수 일치

대명사는 *선행사와 같은 수*.

```text
회피: Each student should bring their book.   (전통 — 잘못)
       *(현대 영어에서는 singular they 허용 — 아래 참고)*
전통: Each student should bring his or her book.
바름: All students should bring their books.   (복수로 변환)
```

### 집합명사

집합명사의 대명사는 *주절의 동사 일치*와 같은 원칙.

```text
미국식: The committee made its decision.   (단수 — its)
영국식: The committee made their decision.  (복수 가능)
```

한 글 안에서 *일관성*.

```text
회피: The team played its best. They won the game.
바름: The team played its best. It won the game.   (단수 일관)
바름: The team played their best. They won the game.   (복수 일관)
```

### 부정대명사

| 부정대명사 | 단복수 |
|---|---|
| each, every, anyone, someone, no one, anybody, everybody, nobody | 단수 |
| both, many, several, few | 복수 |
| all, any, none, some, most | 지칭에 따라 |

```text
전통: Anyone who wishes to attend should bring his or her ID.
현대: Anyone who wishes to attend should bring their ID.
```

## Singular They

전통 문법은 *Anyone … his*를 권했지만 *남성 편향*. *His or her*는 길고 어색.

현대 영어는 *singular they*를 표준으로 인정합니다. *APA 7th edition, Chicago Manual, Merriam-Webster, Oxford Dictionary* 모두 권장.

```text
바름: A good writer revises their work carefully.
바름: Everyone should bring their own laptop.
바름: If a student needs help, they should ask the teacher.
```

### 이점

- 성중립
- 자연스러움
- *he or she*의 길이 회피

### 일치

*singular they*는 *형태상 복수*이므로 동사는 *복수형*.

```text
바름: If anyone calls, tell them I'll be back soon.
바름: They are coming to the meeting alone.   (한 사람 — 복수형 동사)
```

## 인칭 일관성 (Person Shift)

한 글 안에서 *인칭*이 일관되어야 합니다.

```text
회피: When you arrive at a hotel, one should check in immediately.
       (you와 one 혼용)
바름: When you arrive at a hotel, you should check in immediately.
바름: When one arrives at a hotel, one should check in immediately.
```

### 학술 글의 인칭

- **1인칭** *I, we* — 사용 가능 (단, 적절하게)
- **2인칭** *you* — 회피 (수신자가 막연)
- **3인칭** *he, she, they, one, the reader* — 학술 글의 기본

```text
회피: You can see from the data that ...
바름: The data show that ...
바름: As the reader can see from the data, ...
```

## 회피의 대안

### 회피 1: 능동·복수로 변환

```text
회피: A student should turn in his or her assignment on time.
바름: Students should turn in their assignments on time.
```

### 회피 2: 대명사 제거

```text
회피: A student should turn in his or her assignment.
바름: A student should turn in the assignment.
```

### 회피 3: *Singular they*

```text
바름: A student should turn in their assignment.
```

세 가지 모두 유효. 글의 격식·맥락에 따라 선택.

## *Who* vs *Whom* 함정

(Chapter 1.2 복습)

```text
주어 자리 — who
The person who called is my uncle.

목적어 자리 — whom
The person whom I called is my uncle.
The person whom I spoke to is my uncle.
```

*whom*은 현대 영어에서 줄고 있지만 *격식 글*에서는 정확히.

## *Who* vs *That* (사람)

격식 글에서는 사람을 가리킬 때 *who* 선호.

```text
회피 (격식): The man that called is my uncle.
바름: The man who called is my uncle.

회피 (격식): The students that passed will receive certificates.
바름: The students who passed will receive certificates.
```

비격식 글에서는 *that*도 허용.

## 반사대명사 오용

(Chapter 1.2 복습)

```text
회피: Please contact myself with questions.
바름: Please contact me with questions.

회피: John and myself will attend.
바름: John and I will attend.
```

반사대명사는 *주어와 목적어가 같을 때만*.

```text
바름: I taught myself Python.   (주어와 목적어 = I)
바름: She blamed herself.
바름: They consider themselves lucky.
```

## *Its / It's, Whose / Who's, Their / They're*

(Chapter 3.5 복습)

```text
회피: The dog wagged it's tail.
바름: The dog wagged its tail.

회피: Who's book is this?
바름: Whose book is this?

회피: They're house is here.
바름: Their house is here.
```

## 예문

```text
The committee made its decision after extensive discussion. It will be announced tomorrow.
위원회는 광범위한 논의 후 결정을 내렸습니다. 그 결정은 내일 발표될 것입니다.

Everyone who attended the workshop should submit their feedback by Friday.
워크숍에 참석한 모든 사람은 금요일까지 피드백을 제출해야 합니다.

When the manager spoke with the employee, she explained the new policy.
[모호 — 누가 누구에게 설명?]
바름: When the manager spoke with the employee, the manager explained the new policy.

A good researcher always cites their sources carefully.
훌륭한 연구자는 항상 자신의 출처를 신중하게 인용합니다.

The students who completed the assignment received extra credit.
과제를 완료한 학생들은 추가 점수를 받았습니다.
```

## 자주 보는 함정

### 1. 모호한 *she/he/it*

```text
회피: After Tom called Mike, he said he was busy.
       (he = ?)
바름: After Tom called Mike, Mike said he (Mike) was busy.
바름: Tom called Mike to say that he (Tom) was busy.
```

### 2. 막연한 *this/that*

```text
회피: She rejected the proposal. This was unexpected.
바름: She rejected the proposal. This rejection was unexpected.
```

### 3. *Each, every* 복수 대명사 (전통 규칙)

```text
전통: Each student must bring his or her book.
현대: Each student must bring their book.
변환: All students must bring their books.
```

### 4. 인칭 혼용

```text
회피: You should always check your work before one submits it.
바름: You should always check your work before you submit it.
```

### 5. *Who/Whom* 오용

```text
회피: To who did you give the report?
바름: To whom did you give the report?
바름 (구어): Who did you give the report to?
```

### 6. 반사대명사 강조 사용

```text
회피: My team and myself will handle this.
바름: My team and I will handle this.
```

## 정리

- 모든 대명사는 *분명한 선행사*를 가져야 함
- *모호한 참조*는 명사 반복·재구성으로 해결
- *막연한 this/that/it/which* 회피 — 뒤에 명사 붙이기
- 대명사는 *선행사와 수 일치*
- *Singular they*는 현대 영어 표준
- *인칭*은 한 글 안에서 *일관*되게
- 학술 글은 *2인칭 you* 회피
- *반사대명사*는 주어·목적어가 같을 때만
