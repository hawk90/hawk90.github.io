학술 글쓰기에서 *과정 묘사(process description)*는 빈번한 작업입니다. 실험 방법을 설명할 때, 자연 현상의 단계를 묘사할 때, 시스템의 작동을 설명할 때 모두 과정 묘사가 필요합니다. 이 글은 학술 영어의 *수동태 관습*과, *과정 묘사의 흐름*을 만드는 방법을 다룹니다.

## 왜 학술 영어는 수동태를 자주 쓰는가

과정 묘사에서 수동태가 흔한 이유는 *초점*에 있습니다. *과정 자체*에 초점을 맞추려면, *행위자*를 배경으로 밀어 두는 것이 효과적입니다.

### 예 — 독감 백신 제조 과정

```
수동 (과정 중심):
First, the virus strains most likely to cause disease are identified and three
are selected for vaccine development. The virus samples of each selected
strain are injected into separate batches of fertilized eggs to amplify the
amount of virus. Each virus strain is grown separately inside the eggs over
the course of several days, after which it is harvested, inactivated, and
purified. The purified virus strains are then combined to create the vaccine,
blended with a carrier fluid and dispensed into vials.
```

이 단락에서 *who*가 작업을 하는지는 한 번도 명시되지 않습니다. 독자의 주의는 *과정의 단계*에 집중됩니다 — *virus strains*가 *identified, injected, grown, harvested, combined* 됩니다.

### 같은 내용을 능동태로

```
능동 (행위자 중심):
First, researchers identify the virus strains most likely to cause disease and
select three for vaccine development. Technicians inject virus samples of
each selected strain into separate batches of fertilized eggs to amplify the
amount of virus. Technicians grow each virus strain separately inside the
eggs over the course of several days, after which they harvest, inactivate,
and purify it. The technicians then combine the purified virus strains to
create the vaccine, blend it with a carrier fluid and dispense it into vials.
```

같은 정보지만, 이제 *researchers*, *technicians*, *they*가 문장의 주어를 차지합니다. 독자의 주의가 *행위자*로 갑니다. *과정의 단계*가 *배경*으로 밀려납니다.

이 능동 버전은 과정 묘사보다 *직무 분담표(job specification)*에 가까운 인상을 줍니다. 학술 영어에서는 *과정에 초점이 가는* 수동이 더 적합합니다.

## 행위자가 중요할 때

행위자가 *정말 중요한* 자리에서는 능동(또는 *by* + 행위자)을 씁니다.

```
The theory of transformational grammar was first developed by Noam Chomsky.

The Bayesian method has been used by statisticians for many years to aid
decision making on the basis of limited information.
```

*Chomsky*나 *statisticians*가 *누구인가*가 중요한 정보입니다. 분야의 *역사*를 설명하거나 *유명한 연구자*를 인용할 때 *by + 행위자*가 자연스럽습니다.

## *by + 과정* 또는 *by + 비인간 행위자*

학술 영어에서 *by + 사람*보다 *by + 과정*이나 *by + 사물*이 훨씬 흔합니다.

```
The impact velocity can be obtained by calculating the difference of the
arrival times of the two waves.

This enzyme is used by the cancer cells to replicate.

The increased mobility provided by this new joint allows wearers of the
finger prosthesis to hold a cup, to pick up a piece of paper, and in some
cases to write again.

The rate at which heat will be lost by conduction from the body will be
determined by the magnitude of the temperature gradient ... and the
rapidity with which the cooler air in contact with the skin is replaced by
colder air.
```

*by + 과정* (calculating, conduction, replaced by colder air) 또는 *by + 비인간 행위자* (cancer cells, this new joint)이 학술 영어의 전형적 패턴입니다.

## 명령형 — Instructions vs. Process Description

같은 정보를 *지시(instructions)*로 쓸 수도 있고 *과정 묘사*로 쓸 수도 있습니다. 형태가 다릅니다.

### Instructions — 명령형

```
Inoculation of eggs in flu vaccine development

1. Place eggs into egg trays with the blunt end up, and label eggs with
   a specific identification number. Allocate 3 eggs for each specimen.
2. Wipe the blunt end of each egg with 70% ethanol and punch a
   small hole in the shell over the air sac.
3. Aspirate 0.6 ml of processed specimen into a tuberculin syringe
   with a 22 gauge, 1 1/2-inch needle.
4. Hold the egg up to the candler and locate the embryo.
   ...
```

*Place*, *Wipe*, *Aspirate*, *Hold* — 모두 명령형. 독자에게 *직접 지시*를 내립니다.

### Process Description — 수동태

```
First, eggs are placed into egg trays with the blunt end up and labeled
with a specific identification number. Three eggs are allocated for each
specimen. The blunt end of each egg is wiped with 70% ethanol and a
small hole is punched in the shell over the air sac. ...
```

같은 정보지만 이제 *과정 묘사*입니다. *방법(Methods)* 섹션에 들어갈 형태입니다.

### 자기 글에서의 선택

```
지시 → 사용자 매뉴얼, 실험 프로토콜, 요리 레시피
과정 묘사 → 논문의 Methods 섹션, 학술 글의 과정 설명
```

학위 논문에서 자기가 한 실험을 묘사할 때는 *과정 묘사*가 표준입니다.

## Flow of Ideas — 과정 묘사에서 흐름 만들기

여러 단계를 묘사할 때 *문장 사이를 어떻게 잇는지*가 글의 질을 결정합니다. 두 버전을 비교합니다.

### Text A — 약한 흐름

```
❶ Once a bird has been brought to a rehabilitation center, basic
   procedures are followed.
❷ The bird is sedated, if necessary.
❸ The bird is examined to detect broken bones, cuts, or other injuries.
❹ Oil is flushed from its eyes and intestines.
❺ Heavily oiled birds are then wiped with absorbent cloths to remove
   patches of oil.
❻ Stomach-coating medicines may be administered orally to prevent
   additional absorption of oil inside the bird's stomach.
❼ The bird is warmed.
❽ It is placed in a quiet area.
❾ Curtains are hung around the area to limit the bird's contact with
   people.
```

문제 — 각 문장이 *독립적 단계*로 끊어집니다. 시간 부사가 거의 없습니다 (*then* 한 번만). *The bird*가 주어로 반복됩니다.

### Text B — 강한 흐름

```
❶ Once a bird has been brought to a rehabilitation center, basic
   procedures are followed.
❷ First, the bird is sedated, if necessary, and examined to detect broken
   bones, cuts, or other injuries.
❸ Next, oil is flushed from its eyes and intestines.
❹ Heavily oiled birds are then wiped with absorbent cloths to remove
   patches of oil.
❺ Stomach-coating medicines may be administered orally to prevent
   additional absorption of oil inside the bird's stomach.
❻ The bird is then warmed and placed in a quiet area.
❼ Finally, curtains are often hung around the area to limit the bird's
   contact with people.
```

개선 — 시간 부사가 풍부합니다 (*First*, *Next*, *then*, *Finally*). 두 단계가 *and*로 묶입니다 (`sedated ... and examined`, `warmed and placed`). 9 문장이 7 문장으로 줄어 흐름이 매끄럽습니다.

## 시간 부사 — 단계 순서 신호

과정 묘사에 자주 쓰이는 시간 부사:

```
시작:    First, Initially, To begin
순서:    Next, Then, Subsequently, Following this, After this
중간:    During this stage, At this point, Meanwhile
끝:      Finally, Lastly, In the final step
조건:    Once, When, Before, After, As soon as
```

이 부사들이 단락 안에 적절히 분포되어 있으면 독자가 *과정의 어디에 있는지* 항상 압니다.

## *-ed* 분사 활용 — 흐름의 또 다른 도구

*-ed* 분사를 *형용사*로 써서 *직전 단계의 결과*를 다음 문장에 자연스럽게 묶을 수 있습니다.

```
First, the virus strains most likely to cause disease are identified and three
are selected for vaccine development. The virus samples of each selected
strain are injected into separate batches of fertilized eggs to amplify the
amount of virus. Each virus strain is grown separately inside the eggs over
the course of several days, after which it is harvested, inactivated, and
purified. The purified virus strains are then combined to create the vaccine
...
```

*selected* → *selected strain*. *purified* → *purified virus strains*. 직전 문장의 동작이 다음 문장의 *수식어*가 됩니다. *old-to-new* 흐름이 강화됩니다(Unit 1.5 참고).

## Active Voice in Process Descriptions

수동이 흔하지만, 능동이 더 자연스러운 자리도 있습니다.

### 자연 현상 — 인간 개입 없는 과정

```
The Sun rises in the east and sets in the west.
Most metals expand and contract with variations in temperature.
The beam fractures when the load upon it becomes too great.
Tropical storms can form only in areas of high humidity and temperature.
First, the warm sea heats the air above its surface. The warm, moist air
then rises above the sea, creating a center of low pressure.
When demand increases, prices are likely to rise.
```

*자연 과정*은 행위자가 없거나 *자연 자체*입니다. 능동이 더 자연스럽습니다.

### 변화 동사 — *expand, rise, cool, fracture, form, increase*

상태 변화를 가리키는 동사들 — 자동사로 자주 쓰여 *능동*이 적합합니다.

```
The sample temperature increases.
The metal expands.
Population density rises.
The structure fractures under stress.
```

이런 동사를 *수동*으로 만들면 어색합니다 (*The sample temperature is increased* — 행위자가 가해진 경우만).

## 산호초 사례 — SPSE + Process + 능동

다음은 산호초 백화(coral bleaching)에 관한 단락입니다. SPSE 구조 + 과정 묘사 + 능동·수동 혼합의 좋은 예입니다.

```
[Situation]
❶ Coral reef ecosystems are well known for their beauty and diversity.
❷ Found throughout tropical and subtropical regions of the world, they
   are often thought to be the marine equivalent of terrestrial rainforests.

[Problem 도입]
❸ During the last several decades, however, coral reefs have been
   undergoing alarming changes as a result of environmental stresses,
   the most serious of which is whitening or bleaching.

[Process — 능동/수동 혼합]
❹ Although the mechanism of bleaching is not fully understood, this
   phenomenon is linked to the breakdown of the symbiotic relationship
   between the coral and an algae known as zooxanthellae.
❺ The algae, which give the coral its color, live inside the coral and
   perform photosynthesis, sharing the food that they produce.
❻ When the coral is stressed as a result of increases in temperature or
   the amount of light, the zooxanthellae carry out too much
   photosynthesis and in response the coral expels the algae.
❼ The loss of the algae exposes the white calcium carbonate skeletons,
   thus leaving the coral unable to grow or reproduce.
❽ Coral can survive for brief periods of time without the zooxanthellae,
   but if the reef environment does not return to normal, the coral dies.

[Solution + Evaluation]
❾ Coral reefs require from 30 to 100 years to recover from bleaching,
   if they recover at all.
❿ Researchers are now investigating whether this recovery time can be
   accelerated.
⓫ One approach currently under study involves transplanting healthy
   coral into a bleached reef.
⓬ However, thus far, the process has seen limited success.
```

분석 — 능동과 수동이 자연스럽게 섞입니다.

- *live, perform, share, carry out, expels, exposes, dies* — 능동 (생물학적 자연 과정)
- *is linked, is stressed* — 수동 (행위자 불명)
- *are now investigating* — 능동 + 진행 (현재 진행되는 연구)
- *can be accelerated* — 수동 (가속의 가능성을 묻는 것 자체가 초점)

문장마다 *주제가 무엇인지*에 따라 voice가 결정됩니다.

## 정리

- **과정 묘사에 수동태가 흔한 이유** — 과정 자체에 초점이 가게 함
- **행위자가 중요할 때** — 능동 또는 *by + 행위자*
- ***by + 과정* / *by + 비인간 행위자*** 패턴이 학술 영어에 흔함
- **Instructions vs Process Description** — 명령형은 매뉴얼, 수동 묘사는 학술
- **흐름의 도구** — 시간 부사, *-ed* 분사, 연결된 수동
- **자연 과정 / 변화 동사**는 능동이 더 자연스러움
- 한 글 안에 *능동과 수동을 자연스럽게 혼합*

## 한국 학생의 Process 함정

```
1. 모든 동사를 수동태로 — 단조로움
   해결: 자연 과정 → 능동 (rises, increases)
2. 명령형 학술 글에 사용
   회피: "Place the sample..."
   선호: "The sample is placed..."
3. 행위자 누락의 모호함
   "It was decided that..."  ← 누가 결정?
```

## 자기 글 점검

```
□ 자연 과정에 *능동*?
□ 명령형은 *지시문 / 매뉴얼*에만?
□ 행위자가 *모호하지 않은가*?
```
