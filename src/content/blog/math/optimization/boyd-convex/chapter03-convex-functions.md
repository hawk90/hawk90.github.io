> Outline — *Convex function* — `f(θx+(1-θ)y) ≤ θf(x)+(1-θ)f(y)`. *Jensen's inequality*. *1차 조건* — graph가 tangent plane 위. *2차 조건* — Hessian PSD. *대표 예시* — norm·max·log-sum-exp·entropy·log-det. *보존 연산* — nonneg sum·composition·perspective·max·pointwise sup. *Conjugate function* — Legendre-Fenchel. *Quasiconvex·log-concave·log-convex*.
