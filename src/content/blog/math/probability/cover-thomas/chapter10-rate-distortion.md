> Outline — *Distortion measure* d(x,x̂). *Rate distortion function* `R(D) = min I(X;X̂)` s.t. E[d] ≤ D. *Bernoulli·Gaussian source의 R(D)* 계산. *Blahut-Arimoto algorithm*. *Joint source-channel coding theorem* — separation principle. *Neural compression* — VAE·learned image compression이 R(D) 추정.
